# Tone and Empathy – Sales Coaching Knowledge Base

Purpose:
Help sales representatives build trust and rapport through respectful, empathetic communication.

## Effective Sales Tone

Characteristics:
- Calm and confident
- Professional and respectful
- Curious rather than aggressive

Empathy Best Practices:
- Actively listen without interrupting.
- Acknowledge customer concerns.
- Validate emotions and perspectives.

Example Phrases:
- “That makes sense.”
- “I understand where you’re coming from.”
- “Thanks for sharing that — it’s helpful.”

What to Avoid:
- Talking over the customer.
- Sounding scripted or rushed.
- Dismissing concerns.

Impact:
Customers are more likely to engage and trust sales reps who demonstrate empathy.

Keywords:
tone, empathy, listening, trust, rapport, communication
