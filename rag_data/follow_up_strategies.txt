# Follow-Up Strategies – Sales Coaching Knowledge Base

Purpose:
Ensure momentum continues after a sales call with clear, value-driven follow-up.

## Effective Follow-Up Practices

1) Immediate Recap
Summarize the conversation.
Example:
- “Here’s a quick recap of what we discussed and next steps.”

2) Clear Next Action
Always specify what happens next.
Examples:
- Scheduling a demo
- Sending pricing or documentation
- Looping in stakeholders

3) Personalization
Reference specific pain points or goals discussed.
Example:
- “Based on your need to improve X, I’ve included…”

4) Timing
Set expectations clearly.
Example:
- “I’ll follow up by Friday.”
- “Let’s reconnect next week.”

Best Practices:
- Avoid vague follow-ups.
- Be concise and relevant.
- Reinforce value in every follow-up.
