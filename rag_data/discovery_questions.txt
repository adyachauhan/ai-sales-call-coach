# Discovery Questions – Sales Coaching Knowledge Base

Purpose:
Enable sales representatives to understand customer needs, pain points, and goals before pitching a solution.

## Core Discovery Areas

1) Current State
Understand how the customer operates today.
Example Questions:
- “Can you walk me through your current process?”
- “What tools or solutions are you using today?”

2) Pain Points
Identify problems or inefficiencies.
Example Questions:
- “What challenges are you facing with your current setup?”
- “What’s the most frustrating part of this process?”

3) Impact
Understand why the problem matters.
Example Questions:
- “How does this impact your team or customers?”
- “What happens if this issue isn’t resolved?”

4) Success Criteria
Define what a good outcome looks like.
Example Questions:
- “What would success look like for you?”
- “How would you measure improvement?”

5) Decision Process
Understand how decisions are made.
Example Questions:
- “Who else is involved in the decision?”
- “What’s the typical timeline for moving forward?”

Best Practices:
- Ask open-ended questions.
- Listen more than you speak.
- Avoid pitching before understanding the problem.
